The AURCM College Attendance System Website is a dedicated platform developed to efficiently manage student attendance and generate detailed reports for Anna University Regional Campus Madurai. Designed to streamline the attendance process, this system serves as a centralized hub for faculty and students, ensuring transparency and accuracy in attendance management.
Faculty members can utilize the platform to mark daily attendance, track student participation, and generate class-wise or subject-wise attendance reports with just a few clicks. The system also allows for customized reporting, enabling staff to identify students with low attendance and share insights for necessary action.
Students benefit from secure access to their attendance records, where they can view attendance percentages, check monthly or semester-wise summaries, and stay informed about their academic standing. The system also includes alert notifications for students falling short of attendance requirements, ensuring they remain proactive in maintaining their eligibility.
Built with scalability and reliability in mind, the platform integrates seamlessly with existing academic workflows and provides role-based access control for secure data handling. By digitizing and automating attendance management, the AURCM College Attendance System enhances administrative efficiency, reduces paperwork, and fosters better communication between faculty and students.
